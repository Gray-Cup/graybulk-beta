# Gray Bulk — Platform Definition & Positioning

## Core Positioning

**Gray Bulk** is an Indian B2B commerce infrastructure platform that enables suppliers, wholesalers, manufacturers, importers, exporters, distributors, and industrial businesses to sell products online with automated settlements, low transaction fees, and a scalable supplier storefront system.

Think:

* B2B-first Amazon for India
* Supplier-led marketplace
* Built for wholesale transactions
* Infrastructure-first commerce platform
* Fast onboarding, automated payouts, scalable catalogs

---

# Internal Product Description (for AI agents)

Gray Bulk is an India-focused B2B marketplace platform where suppliers, wholesalers, manufacturers, and distributors can create business profiles, list products, receive orders, and accept payments with automated settlements. The platform supports scalable supplier storefronts with tiered product listing plans and monetizes through annual supplier subscriptions and transaction fees. The tone of the platform should feel industrial, efficient, modern, trustworthy, and infrastructure-driven rather than consumer-oriented.
